Humble Beginnings

Quatro first was started in 1982 in the United Kingdom, as a carbonated fruit juice blend of pineapple, orange, passion fruit and grapefruit. These four fruits gave the Quatro juice its name

First History Image

Image

Throughout the 80s

During the 1980s, Quatro drinks were sold in stores from 1982 until 1989. The drink was later featured in an episode of the British reality TV competition series “The Apprentice” for a task where teams had to create their own soft drink brand, with the Quatro brand being used to give the teams inspiration for their brands
